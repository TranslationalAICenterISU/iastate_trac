TrAC Training on Intro to Cloud-based Deep Learning

The TrAC @ Iowa State University along with CyVerse.org will host a day long hybrid workshop on Introduction to Cloud-based Deep Learning. This workshop will introduce you to concepts in cloud, containerization, and GPU computing with Python. We will demonstrate a simple deep learning application problem using public research cyberinfrastructure with version control systems and containerization.

Agenda

Intro Workshop Days are at the following times:

  1. Part 1 10:00–13:00 US Central Daylight Time (11:00–14:00 US Eastern Daylight Time)
  2. Part 2 14:00–17:00 US Central Daylight Time (15:00–18:00 US Eastern Daylight Time)
